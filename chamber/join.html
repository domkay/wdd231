NP Membership Benefits

  • No membership fee: Perfect for non-profit organizations with limited budgets.
  • Access to networking events: Connect with other non-profits and community leaders.
  • Listing in the Chamber directory: Increase your visibility and reach within the community.
  • Monthly newsletters: Stay informed about local opportunities and resources.
  • Volunteer opportunities: Engage with the community and make a difference.

Who is it for? Non-profit organizations, charities, and community groups.

×

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Access to networking events: Build relationships with local businesses and professionals.
  • Discounts on Chamber events: Save on tickets to workshops, seminars, and conferences.
  • Listing in the Chamber directory: Showcase your business to potential customers.
  • Business resources: Access templates, guides, and tools to grow your business.
  • Social media shoutouts: Get featured on the Chamber's social media platforms.

Who is it for? Small businesses and startups looking to establish themselves in the community.

×

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits: Everything included in the Bronze membership.
  • Free advertising in the Chamber newsletter: Reach a wider audience with your promotions.
  • Access to exclusive training sessions: Learn from industry experts and improve your skills.
  • Priority event registration: Secure your spot at popular events before they sell out.
  • Business mentorship: Get paired with experienced mentors to guide your growth.

Who is it for? Established businesses looking to expand their reach and influence.

×

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits: Everything included in the Silver membership.
  • Spotlight position on the Chamber homepage: Get featured prominently on our website.
  • VIP access to Chamber events: Enjoy exclusive perks at events, including reserved seating and complimentary refreshments.
  • Annual business review: Receive a comprehensive analysis of your business performance.
  • Custom marketing package: Work with our team to create a tailored marketing strategy.

Who is it for? Large businesses and industry leaders seeking maximum visibility and influence.
